Bandwidth Aggregator

Description

Duration: January 2018 to Till date

Team size: 6

Problem Statement: The project is part of Smart India Hackathon 2018 initiative by Government of India where ‘Develop device to aggregate internet bandwidth of 2/3/4 G connectivity, alternate use of SIM’s from different service providers’. For more details please visit Smart India Hackathon 2018

Solution: We will create a device which can take two or more internet connections as input and generates an aggregated bandwidth of all the inputs. Example, if one input is providing 1.5 MB/s of speed and the other is providing 2.5 MB/s of speed, then the output will be approximately 4 MB/s of speed which can be then used through Wi-Fi connectivity.

Progress:

The solution we proposed was selected for the Level 2, where we have to prepare a prototype of it. So currently, we are preparing a prototype of the device.
